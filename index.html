About Me

I am a Software Engineer at Google. My work focuses on developing infrastructure and datasets for evaluating the accuracy of AI agents that perform complex tasks and use various tools and resources.

I completed my PhD at the School of Computing and Data Science at the University of Illinois, Urbana‑Champaign in May 2024. My advisor was Associate Professor Sasa Misailovic. I was previously a Research Intern at Microsoft Research India, where I worked on fixed-point training of RNNs. I graduated with honours from the Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad in 2017.
